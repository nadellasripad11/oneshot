# OneShot Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started in 2 Minutes

### Step 1: Go to the App
Open **https://oneshot-chi-nine.vercel.app** in your browser.

### Step 2: Create a Task
1. Click **"New Task"** in the sidebar
2. Describe what you want (e.g., "Write a business plan for an AI startup")
3. Pick a category (optional but helps)
4. Click **Execute**

### Step 3: Get Results
Watch the progress timeline — it'll show you what's happening step by step. Once done, you'll see your result with options to:
- **Copy** — Quick copy to clipboard (turns green when done)
- **Download** — Save as a .txt file
- **Share** — Share with others
- **Create Another** — Go back and make another task

That's it! 🎉

## 🎯 Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific** — "Write a marketing strategy" works better than "help with marketing"
2. **Add Context** — "Write a business plan for an AI note-taking app targeting students" is better than just "business plan"
3. **Pick a Category** — It helps OneShot understand what you need
4. **One Goal at a Time** — Don't ask for 10 things; ask for one thing, get it, then ask for the next

## ❓ FAQ

**How long does a task take?**
30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on complexity. Shorter tasks are faster.

**Can I use the results commercially?**
Yes! Whatever you create is yours to use, sell, or modify.

**What if I don't like the result?**
Try rephrasing your request or adding more context. AI gets better with clarity.

**Is my data private?**
Your tasks are processed through our system but not stored long-term or used for training.

**Can I integrate this with my tools?**
Not yet, but it's coming! For now, download or copy your results and use them wherever.
